Saving Lives

Blood Bank

Our dedicated blood bank serves the healthcare needs of Hosur and surrounding regions, ensuring a reliable supply of safe blood for those in need.

8,000+ Units Collected

Thousands of blood units collected through regular donation camps and walk-in donors, saving countless lives across the region.

Regular Donation Camps

We organize blood donation drives across Hosur partnering with corporates, colleges, and community groups to maintain healthy blood reserves.

Hospital Partnerships

Supplying blood to hospitals across Hosur and neighboring districts, ensuring timely availability during emergencies and surgeries.

Donor Network

A robust network of voluntary blood donors who are ready to respond to urgent requests for rare and common blood groups.

Quality & Safety

Strict adherence to blood safety protocols with proper testing, storage, and handling to ensure every unit meets quality standards.

Free for the Needy

Blood is provided free of cost to economically weaker sections, ensuring no life is lost due to lack of resources.

Building for the Future

Charitable Trust

Our registered charitable trust ensures the long-term sustainability of our service projects, providing a legal and transparent framework for all community initiatives.

Service Hall Project

Over 40 Lakhs invested in building a permanent community service hall that will serve as the hub for all charitable activities in Hosur. This multipurpose facility will host medical camps, educational programs, community events, and emergency relief coordination.

Education Sponsorships

Sponsoring students from underprivileged backgrounds through school and college, covering fees, books, uniforms, and supplies to ensure no child is denied education due to financial constraints.

Medical Aid Fund

Providing financial assistance for surgeries, treatments, and ongoing medical care for those who cannot afford it, particularly supporting cataract surgeries and cancer treatment.

School Infrastructure

Over 50 Lakhs invested in the adopted Moranapalli Government School, building classrooms, labs, restrooms, and providing furniture, computers, and learning materials.
